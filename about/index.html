About

Gabriel Martinez is an experienced software developer, programming language polyglot, tinkerer, and gamer. He is the original author of CU2CL, a translator from CUDA to OpenCL. He currently works at Super Evil Megacorp, and in his spare time obsesses over Rust, climbs rocks, and makes games.
