Who is This?

My name is Gabe Martinez. I'm a software developer, gamer, and Linux hobbyist. This is where I talk about my current projects and inflict my opinion of indie games on the rest of the Internet. I'm pretty good at code if you want to pay me for it. I'm also available for gaming-related shenanigans on Steam.

About

Hey there! If you often find yourself wondering, “Just who is this Gabe person?” well, you’ve come to the right place! This Gabe person is about equal parts gamer and developer. As a gamer, he has a penchant for roguelikes, action-adventure à la The Legend of Zelda or Dark Souls, along with the occasional puzzle game. He is currently playing Guacamelee and The Swapper, while impatiently waiting for Dark Souls 2 on the PC. As a developer, he mainly keeps to desktop Linux, writing what he can in Python and delving into C and C++ as necessary. He is currently working on a slew of projects: games, a Notational Velocity clone, even this web site!

Now, hopefully this has satisfied your thirst for knowledge. But be ready, as there is more to come soon…
